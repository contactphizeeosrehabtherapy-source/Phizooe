About PhiZeeo

We are one of Chennai’s 5-Star Rated Home Physiotherapy Services, focused on helping people Heal Comfortably at Home. Our mission is to deliver professional, personalized physiotherapy through expert home visits.

Our physiotherapy and physical training programs include advanced assessment, surgery-less remedies, and drug-free treatments for Orthopedic conditions, Neurological disorders (Pediatric & Geriatric), Sports injuries, and Cardiopulmonary cases.

Dr.Zeenith VR and Dr.Josephine are highly experienced and dedicated physiotherapist with deep expertise in home-visit practice since 2017. They personally guides and supports every patient based on their unique needs.

We proudly provide treatment and training for all age groups and have successfully treated and trained numerous patients and clients across Chennai.
